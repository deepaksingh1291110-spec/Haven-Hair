Features

Built for both sides.

🗺️

Live Map

See every open barbershop near you with real-time queue counts and wait times on an interactive map.

🪙

Virtual Token

Grab a walk-in token without going to the shop. Track your queue position from anywhere.

🔔

Smart Alerts

Get notified when you're 2 customers away. No more guessing, no more wasted time.

📅

Advance Booking

Reserve your slot in advance. Pick your barber, pick your time, show up ready.

⭐

Verified Reviews

Only real customers who booked through Haven Hair can leave reviews — no fake ratings.

🔍

Smart Filters

Filter by distance, rating, availability, or specialty. Find exactly what you need fast.

👆

2-Button System

Your entire workflow is two taps. Customer Sits → Haircut Done. Nothing more, nothing less.

🔁

Auto Queue

Queue manages itself. Tokens issue automatically, wait times update instantly for all customers.

🛡️

No-Show Protection

If a booked slot passes without being started, AI auto-skips it and moves the queue forward.

💬

Customer Outreach

AI flags unhappy customers and nudges you to reach out. You write the message — in your own words.

📊

Weekly Reports

Every week, a full performance summary — complaints, happy customers, reputation change, AI insights.

🏅

Reputation Score

AI scores your shop and awards badges. Top Rated shops appear first in search — automatically.
